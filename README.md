* Zudio Annual Sales Analysis Dashboard *


Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Excel dashboard analyzing the annual sales performance of Zudio. The dashboard provides actionable insights into customer behavior, sales trends, and regional performance, enabling data-driven decision-making for business strategy and operations.


Key Visualizations

The dashboard includes the following visualizations:

Sales vs Orders – Shows the correlation between the number of orders and total sales to identify high-performing periods.

Men vs Women Purchases – Compares the purchasing behavior of male and female customers to determine which gender contributes more to sales.

Order Status – Displays the distribution of orders based on their current status (e.g., delivered, pending, canceled) to track fulfillment performance.

Top 5 States by Sales – Highlights the states generating the highest revenue to identify key markets.

Orders by Gender – Provides insight into the volume of orders placed by male vs female customers.

Sales by Channels – Analyzes revenue contribution from different sales channels (e.g., online, in-store) to optimize channel strategy.



Tools Used

Microsoft Excel – For data analysis, visualization, and dashboard creation.

Excel Functions & Features – Pivot Tables, Charts, Conditional Formatting, and Slicers.



Dataset

The dashboard is based on Zudio’s annual sales data, which includes:

Order details

Customer information (gender)

Sales amount

Order status

Location (state)

Sales channels



How to Use

Open the Excel file.

Navigate through the dashboard tabs to explore visualizations.

Use slicers or filters to interact with the data and focus on specific metrics, time periods, or customer segments.

However charts to see detailed insights.


Insights

Identify which gender contributes most to sales and orders.

Monitor order fulfillment and pending orders for operational efficiency.

Understand which regions and channels generate the highest revenue.

Make data-driven decisions for marketing and inventory management.


Conclusion

This dashboard provides a clear, interactive, and actionable view of Zudio’s annual sales, helping stakeholders track performance, spot trends, and optimize business strategies effectively.
